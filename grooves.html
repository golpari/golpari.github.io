Grooves
Ann Arbor's Local Music Hub
Background
Ann Arbor has a diverse music scene, with plenty of artists both small and big coming through the area. Often, small gigs are held at local bars and venues, and they are advertised by either the performers or the venues themselves.

Without a platform for scheduling, music patrons in the area have no real way to know what performances and gigs are going on at any one time. Grooves provides a platform for venues, artists, and locals to collaborate and store the current gigs that are in the area.
Work
Grooves' intention is to allow users to log into personal accounts that represent either an artist (eg. a band), a venue, or simply a patron. These accounts all have access to a shared editable calendar. Users accounts can add events that they are either hosting, performing in, or have simply been heard about.

The login and registration system of the app uses Google Firebase to securely store all user information. Firebase is also used to store calendar events to a cloud so that all users can stay updated.

The app was developed in Flutter with a team of 2 developers and a project manager to support both Android and iOS functionality. Our work is intended to one day expand to include more specialized Calendar edits. It is intended that venues and artists can confirm or cancel events should their details change, and that venues/artists can put out campaigns to book other artists or instrumentalists.
